Rowing

Rowing, or crew, is the sport of racing boats. Rowing is divided into two categories: sculling and sweep rowing. In sculling, each rower holds one oar in each hand, while in sweep rowing each rower holds one oar with both hands. There are several racing categories in rowing: single sculls, double sculls and pairs, quad sculling and fours, and eights. Most elite and championship level racing is held on a 2 kilometre long course.
